About
We believe learning technology should be as simple as opening a browser.

Workshop-DIY is a French nonprofit (loi 1901) that teaches young people robotics, artificial intelligence, electronics, 3D printing, programming, and cybersecurity — all through browser-based apps that require zero installation.

Our ecosystem: 39 free web applications across 7 educational domains. From driving robots via Bluetooth to training AI models to ethical hacking sandboxes — all running in a browser tab.
