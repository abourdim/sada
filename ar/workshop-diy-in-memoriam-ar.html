In Memoriam

الذين فقدناهم في خدمة التعليم
🔌
USB-C Cable #1
أكتوبر 2025 — فبراير 2026
Faithful companion to the ESP32. متصل without complaint. Gave its life on Workbench 7 when pulled sideways at an acute angle during an enthusiastic code upload.
Cause: Internal conductor fatigue · Manner: Student-induced misadventure
Survived by: 6 remaining USB-C cables in the drawer
💡
WS2812B LED #17
مارس 2024 — نوفمبر 2025
Burned brighter than any LED in the strip. Literally. Received 5.5V instead of 5V due to a wiring error. Died in a blaze of white. The adjacent LEDs still mourn — they display blue every Tuesday in tribute.
Cause: Overvoltage (5.5V applied to 5V rated component)
Survived by: 59 remaining LEDs in the strip
🔋
18650 Battery "Old Reliable"
يناير 2024 — أغسطس 2025
Powered bit-bot through 47 سقطات المكتب and 2,000 line-following missions. Capacity gradually declined from 2600mAh to 800mAh. Retired with honors when it could no longer power a single motor. It gave everything.
Cause: Natural cell degradation after 500+ charge cycles
Survived by: bit-bot (who misses the extra range)
🔧
SG90 Servo "Spinny"
يونيو 2024 — يناير 2026
Spent 18 months turning the ultrasonic sensor left and right. Never complained. Developed a 0.42° drift per hour that became its signature quirk. One day, the gear stripped. The sound was unmistakable. The silence that followed was worse.
Cause: Gear strip after 400,000+ rotations
Survived by: Replacement servo "Spinny II"
🖥️
The Old Laptop
2018 — ديسمبر 2025
Served as the primary demo machine for three years. Could run Chrome with only 12 tabs before the fan sounded like a jet engine. Died during a Docker workshop when it tried to build three containers simultaneously. It tried its best. That's all we can ask.
Cause: Thermal shutdown (final) · 47 Chrome tabs open at time of death
Survived by: A shiny new laptop (16GB RAM) that doesn't know the struggle
🔥
Hot Glue Stick Collection (Jan 2024 batch)
يناير 2024 — consumed gradually through 2025
24 sticks. Gone too soon. Melted in service to robot chassis construction, sensor mounting, and what one instructor called "structural engineering." Each stick gave its molecular integrity so that a child's robot could drive another day.
Cause: Thermoplastic sacrifice (350°C)
Survived by: A new 50-pack (arriving Tuesday)
📡
HC-SR04 Ultrasonic Sensor "Echo"
مارس 2024 — سبتمبر 2025
Measured distances faithfully for 18 months. Reported obstacles at 2cm to 400cm. One day, started reporting everything as 0cm. The world, to Echo, had closed in completely. We replaced it, but we never forgot.
Cause: Transducer membrane failure
Survived by: "Echo II" (who seems nervous)
