🏆 WORKSHOP-DIY VERDICT

ESP32-C3 wins when: You need WiFi, more processing power, IoT projects, advanced students, tight budget (€3 vs €15). Used in Workshop-DIY's esp32-lab for BLE→micro:bit communication, IoT dashboards, and as bit-bot's brain when micro:bit isn't available.
Micro:bit wins when: Teaching beginners, needing built-in sensors (no extra wiring!), visual feedback (LED matrix), block programming, younger students (6+). Used in Workshop-DIY's bit-bot, RXY, bit-playground, teachable-machine, bitmoji-lab, and 4 more apps.
Together they're unstoppable: ESP32-C3 as BLE client talks to micro:bit BLE server. ESP32 handles WiFi/IoT while micro:bit handles sensors/display. Workshop-DIY uses both: micro:bit as the kid-facing controller, ESP32 as the behind-the-scenes powerhouse.
Workshop-DIY app count: micro:bit: 9 apps (bit-bot, rxy, talking-robot, teachable-machine, face-tracking, bitmoji-lab, bit-playground, usb-logger, ble-logger) · ESP32: 1 app (esp32-lab) + firmware for bit-bot motor control.
