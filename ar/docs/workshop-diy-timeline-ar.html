A thought: "What if kids could control robots from a browser?" First lines of code written. Coffee consumed: immeasurable.

🤖 bit-bot v1.0
First app launched: browser-based robot control via Web Bluetooth. A micro:bit moves. A child screams with joy. Everything changes.
APP LAUNCH

📋 Association Founded
Workshop-DIY officially registered as Association Loi 1901 in France. Paperwork: 3 weeks. Coffee during paperwork: 47 cups.

MediaPipe hand tracking goes live. Kids wave at screens for hours. Parents confused but supportive.
APP LAUNCH

First in-person workshop held. 12 students. 3 robots. 1 accidental table flip. Zero injuries (barely).

A mysterious bug appears in bit-bot's BLE reconnection logic. After 3 weeks of debugging, it is officially declared "a feature."
