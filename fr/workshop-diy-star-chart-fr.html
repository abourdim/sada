LE GRAND BOT
Roboticus Majoris
The largest constellation. Its two brightest stars form the wheels. Visible year-round. Falls below the horizon 47 times per year.

Insectum XLII
Visible uniquement à 3h42. No known origin. Appears in all star charts despite never being catalogued. Some say it was here before the universe.

Contains other stars inside it. Parents mistake it for Aquarius. Their 14-year-old children correctly identify it every time.
