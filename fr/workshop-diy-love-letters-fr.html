Eternally undefined,

My beloved Chassis,

I know I'm not the most elegant bonding agent. I'm not solder. I'm not epoxy. I'm not even super glue. I'm hot glue — melted at 200°C, applied in wobbly strings, hardened in seconds, ugly as sin.

But I held. Drop 1, I held. Drop 12, I held. Drop 31, I held. Drop 47? Still holding.

Every time gravity tries to take you apart, I am there. Between every seam, under every servo mount, around every wire. Invisible, imperfect, indispensable.

The students laugh at my strings. Let them laugh. When the robot hits the floor and the wheels still spin and the BLE still broadcasts — that's not luck. That's love, applied at 200 degrees Celsius.
Permanently bonded,
Hot Glue 🔥
