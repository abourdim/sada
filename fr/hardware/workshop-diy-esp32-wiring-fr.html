⚠️ WIRING NOTES

🔴 5V logic: NeoPixels and servo need 5V. ESP32-C3 GPIO is 3.3V — NeoPixels usually work, but add a level shifter for reliability.
⚡ Power: Don't power motors from ESP32 USB! Use separate battery via L298N. Partager GND between all boards.
📌 Pin conflict: GPIO0/1 used for ADC (IR sensors) — don't use for servo! Servo on GPIO10 to avoid conflicts.
🔥 Hot glue: All wires secured with colle chaude. Structural grade. Has survived 47 chutes de bureau. Ne pas question the engineering.
