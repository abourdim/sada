In Memoriam

CEUX QUE NOUS AVONS PERDUS AU SERVICE DE L'ÉDUCATION
🔌
USB-C Cable #1
Octobre 2025 — Février 2026
Compagnon fidèle de l'ESP32. Connecté sans se plaindre. A donné sa vie sur l'Établi 7 when pulled sideways at an acute angle during an enthusiastic code upload.
Cause : Fatigue interne des conducteurs · Circonstance : Mésaventure causée par un élève
Survécu par : 6 câbles USB-C restants dans le tiroir
💡
WS2812B LED #17
Mars 2024 — Novembre 2025
A brillé plus que toute autre LED in the strip. Literally. Received 5.5V instead of 5V due to a wiring error. Died in a blaze of white. The adjacent LEDs still mourn — they display blue every Mardi in tribute.
Cause: Overvoltage (5.5V applied to 5V rated component)
Survécu par : 59 remaining LEDs in the strip
🔋
18650 Battery "Old Reliable"
Janvier 2024 — Août 2025
Powered bit-bot through 47 chutes de bureau and 2,000 line-following missions. Capacity gradually declined from 2600mAh to 800mAh. Retired with honors when it could no longer power a single motor. Il a tout donné.
Cause: Dégradation naturelle de la cellule after 500+ charge cycles
Survécu par : bit-bot (who misses the extra range)
🔧
SG90 Servo "Spinny"
Juin 2024 — Janvier 2026
Spent 18 months turning the ultrasonic sensor left and right. Never complained. Developed a 0.42° drift per hour that became its signature quirk. One day, the gear stripped. The sound was unmistakable. The silence that followed was worse.
Cause: Gear strip after 400,000+ rotations
Survécu par : Servo de remplacement "Spinny II"
🖥️
The Old Laptop
2018 — Décembre 2025
Served as the primary demo machine for three years. Could run Chrome with only 12 tabs before the fan sounded like a jet engine. Died during a Docker workshop when it tried to build three containers simultaneously. It tried its best. That's all we can ask.
Cause: Thermal shutdown (final) · 47 Chrome tabs open at time of death
Survécu par : A shiny new laptop (16GB RAM) that doesn't know the struggle
🔥
Colle chaude Stick Collection (Jan 2024 batch)
Janvier 2024 — consumed gradually through 2025
24 sticks. Gone too soon. Melted in service to robot chassis construction, sensor mounting, and what one instructor called "structural engineering." Each stick gave its molecular integrity so that a child's robot could drive another day.
Cause: Thermoplastic sacrifice (350°C)
Survécu par : A new 50-pack (arriving Mardi)
📡
HC-SR04 Ultrasonic Sensor "Echo"
Mars 2024 — Septembre 2025
Measured distances faithfully for 18 months. Reported obstacles at 2cm to 400cm. One day, started reporting everything as 0cm. The world, to Echo, had closed in completely. We replaced it, but we never forgot.
Cause: Transducer membrane failure
Survécu par : "Echo II" (who seems nervous)
